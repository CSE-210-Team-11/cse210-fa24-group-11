About Streek

Streek takes a refreshingly honest approach to the universal challenge of procrastination. Unlike traditional productivity apps that preach motivation and willpower, Streek acknowledges the reality that studying and staying focused is genuinely difficult for most people.

Founded by the HackStreet Boys in 2024, this app embraces the human tendency to delay, procrastinate, and feel overwhelmed, while providing practical tools to help users maintain their integrity despite these natural inclinations.

Rather than trying to transform users into productivity machines, Streek meets them where they are—acknowledging their struggles, tears, and complaints—while gently pushing them toward their goals.
